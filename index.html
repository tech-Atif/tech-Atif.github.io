About

I began by tinkering with circuits and microcontrollers at Desh Bhagat University while pursuing Electrical Engineering. There, I took my first steps into teaching—running workshops and mentoring peers in Robotics and AI. Since then, I’ve helped learners build line-followers, obstacle-avoiders, IoT prototypes, and AI mini-projects—always keeping electronics at the core.
